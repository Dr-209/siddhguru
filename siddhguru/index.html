About Ashram

Enriched and adorned by the rigorous Sadhana, meditation, and
austerities observed by Sri Siddhguru, The Brahmrishi Ashram has
become a sacred sanctuary where the divine presence of gods is
palpable. Here, you can feel a distinct positive aura that permeates the
soil and surroundings. This is the place where hearts can let go of their
worries & troubles, and experience 'the eternal bliss'.
